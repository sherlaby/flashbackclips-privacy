Flashback Clips – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026
App name: Flashback Clips
Developer contact: sherlaby2dd@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how Flashback Clips (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use our Android application published on Google Play. The App is operated by an individual developer. If you have questions, contact us at the email above.

Summary

  • Your recordings stay on your device. Clips you capture (screen replay videos and optional internal-audio recordings) are stored in the App’s private library on your phone. We do not operate servers to collect or store your recording files.
  • The App uses the internet for showing ads (Google AdMob), processing in-app purchases (Google Play Billing), and communicating with Google Play services. Using these features does not upload your clip files to us.
  • No user account is required to use the core features of the App.

Information we process

Content you create (stored locally)

When you use backtrack screen capture or internal audio capture, the App saves files to your device (private app storage). You may export clips to the system gallery or music library, share them via other apps, or delete them inside the App. We do not receive copies of these files.

Information processed via third-party services

When you use ads or purchase Pro features, Google may process data according to its own policies, which may include:

  • Advertising ID or similar device identifiers (AdMob)
  • Purchase and billing records (Google Play)
  • Technical data needed to deliver ads or complete transactions

We do not sell your personal information. For details, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob & advertising

Permissions and why they are used

  • Display over other apps (overlay): floating controls and tool menu while you use other apps.
  • Screen capture (MediaProjection): only after you approve the system dialog; used for backtrack video and for internal audio capture (playback capture).
  • Notifications: foreground service status while recording or buffering; actions such as save/stop during backtrack.
  • Record audio: used for Android playback capture only; the App does not record microphone / ambient voice for internal-audio mode as described in the App.
  • Vibrate: optional haptic feedback for actions.

How you control your data

  • Delete clips in the in-app library.
  • Export or share only when you choose to.
  • Uninstalling the App removes app-private data from your device (subject to Android system behavior).
  • Reset advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device Google settings.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Legal use

You must use the App lawfully and with respect for others’ privacy and copyright. Do not use the App to record others without consent where required by law.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Flashback Clips – 隐私政策 （中文）

最后更新：2026年5月30日
应用名称：回溯高光 / Flashback Clips
开发者联系：sherlaby2dd@gmail.com

本政策说明您使用 Flashback Clips（「本应用」）时，开发者如何处理相关信息。本应用由个人开发者运营。如有疑问，请通过上述邮箱联系我们。

概要

  • 您的录制内容保存在本机。 回溯录屏、内录音频等片段保存在应用私有库中，我们不会通过自有服务器收集或存储您的录制文件。
  • 本应用会使用网络，用于展示广告（Google AdMob）、应用内购买（Google Play 结算）及与 Google Play 服务通信。上述用途不会将您的片段文件上传给我们。
  • 使用核心功能无需注册账号。

我们处理的信息

您创建的内容（本地存储）

使用回溯录屏或内录音频时，文件保存在设备上（应用私有存储）。您可自行导出到系统相册/音乐库、通过其他应用分享，或在应用内删除。我们不会收到这些文件的副本。

通过第三方服务处理的信息

当您观看广告或购买 Pro 功能时，Google 可能按其隐私政策处理数据，例如：

  • 广告 ID 或类似设备标识（AdMob）
  • 购买与账单记录（Google Play）
  • 提供广告或完成交易所需的技术数据

我们不会出售您的个人信息。详见 Google 隐私政策 及 AdMob 相关说明。

权限及用途

  • 悬浮窗/显示在其他应用上层： 在其他应用上方显示录制控件与菜单。
  • 录屏（MediaProjection）： 仅在您同意系统对话框后使用；用于回溯视频及内录（播放捕获）。
  • 通知： 录制或缓存期间的前台服务提示；回溯时通知栏保存/停止等操作。
  • 录音权限： 用于系统「播放捕获」，内录模式下不采集麦克风/环境人声（以应用内说明为准）。
  • 震动： 操作反馈。

您如何管理数据

  • 在应用「我的片段」中删除条目。
  • 仅在您主动操作时导出或分享。
  • 卸载应用将清除应用私有数据（以 Android 系统行为为准）。
  • 可在系统 Google 设置中重置广告 ID 或关闭个性化广告。

儿童

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童（或您所在司法辖区规定的最低年龄）。我们不会故意收集儿童个人信息。

合法使用

请依法使用本应用，尊重他人隐私与著作权。在法律要求征得同意的情况下，请勿未经授权录制他人。
